Tips for Maintaining Your Golf Cart Tires

Proper maintenance extends the life of your tires and ensures safety:

  • Check tire pressure regularly and inflate to recommended levels.
  • Inspect tires for cracks, punctures, or worn tread.
  • Rotate tires periodically to ensure even wear.
  • Clean tires regularly to prevent buildup of dirt and debris.
