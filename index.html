Tips to Maintain Your Golf Cart Tires

  • Check tire pressure regularly and maintain recommended levels.
  • Inspect tires for cracks, punctures, or uneven wear.
  • Rotate tires periodically to ensure even tread wear.
  • Clean tires to remove dirt and debris for better grip.
